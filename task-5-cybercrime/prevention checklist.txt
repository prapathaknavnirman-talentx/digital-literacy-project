Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist


• Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone  
• Do not accept unknown payment requests  
• Always verify the sender before making transactions  
• Avoid clicking on suspicious links  
• Use strong passwords and enable security features  
• Regularly check your bank statements  
• Use trusted and official apps only  
• Be cautious of calls pretending to be from banks  


UPI Safety Tips:


• Remember: Entering UPI PIN means money is being sent, not received  
• Do not scan unknown QR codes  


Report Cybercrime:


• National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in  
• Helpline Number: 1930
